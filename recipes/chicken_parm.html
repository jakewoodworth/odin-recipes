Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American comfort dish made with breaded chicken cutlets that are pan-fried until golden and topped with warm marinara sauce and melted cheese. This version focuses on simple ingredients and straightforward steps to create a crispy, flavorful meal without unnecessary complexity.

The chicken is coated in seasoned breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese, giving it a crunchy exterior while staying juicy inside. Finished in the oven, the dish brings together savory tomato sauce, gooey melted mozzarella, and fresh herbs, making it perfect for a hearty weeknight dinner or a casual gathering.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds chicken tenderloins
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided
  • ¾ teaspoons freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 1½ cups panko
  • ¾ cup plus 2 tablespoons grated Parmigiano Reggiano, divided

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare a baking dish.
  2. Pound the chicken breasts to an even thickness and season them.
  3. Coat the chicken in a mixture of breadcrumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Cook the chicken in a pan until golden on both sides.
  5. Transfer the chicken to the baking dish and add marinara sauce.
  6. Top with mozzarella and extra Parmesan.
  7. Bake until the cheese is melted and the chicken is fully cooked.
  8. Serve warm, optionally with pasta or vegetables.
